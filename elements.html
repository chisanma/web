Neuschwanstein Castle

The castle was built in the 19th century as a hobby of King Ludwig II of Bavaria. It is located near the Austrian border. You can visit the castle, but you have to buy a tour ticket at the ticket office at the foot of the castle. It is a five-hour drive from Leipzig, where you will be staying for your studies abroad.
