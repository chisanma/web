Place Details

Neuschwanstein Castle

The castle is said to be a model of Cinderella Castle of Disneyland ✨The castle was built in the 19th century as a hobby of King Ludwig II of Bavaria. It is located near the Austrian border. You can visit the castle, but you have to buy a tour ticket at the ticket office at the foot of the castle. It is a five-hour drive from Leipzig, where you will be staying for your studies abroad.

Hohenzollern Castle

The castle looks like it's floating in the sky when it's foggy. It is popular for its Harry Potter-like atmosphere👦⚡🐍

SWU Boston

This is the campus of Showa Women's University in Boston.From here it is about an hour by train and foot to downtown Boston. Manhattan is also only a four-hour train ride away, making it perfect for a weekend trip.

Trinity Church

This is said to be the most beautiful church in Boston.It is only a fifty minute walk and train ride from the school in Boston. It has been selected as one of the "Ten Most Significant Buildings in the United States" since 1885, when the list began.

Ante interdum

This is the SWU garden. There are benches around a big tree, round in shape and very relaxing. It is illuminated at night, and is especially beautiful in winter with snow.

This is the cafeteria. I often see students eating here, especially in the summer. Even though this university is located in the city, it is a place where you can experience nature.

This is the street next to the "British School in Tokyo," a school located inside Showa Women's University. There are many students from the British School here during lunch time. This is where you can experience the atmosphere of being abroad.
