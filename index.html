Major

English

I have six English classes a week. Each class is 1.5 hours long, so I have 540 minutes,9 hours of English class per week. Four of those classes are English only.

German

German classes are held four times a week by four different teachers. However, it is divided into two parts. One is taught by a native speaker. This is mostly conversation and communication. The other class is taught by a Japanese teacher and focuses on grammar. This is one of my hardest classes.

International Studies

There is usually one of these classes every semester. In the first semester of my freshman year, we had a Intercultural Communication class, where we learned about the things that are important in communicating with people from different cultures.Other classes to learn about international situations such as refugees and immigration are also included in this section.

Others

All other classes are included here. For example, the class in which I learned how to make this website is called Media Communication. There are also many other classes in this section that you can choose from, such as a class on religion.
