About Me

This photo is me with the sunrise. My friend and I took this photo at the sea in our hometown, Amami Oshima.

My Life

I was born in a hospital in Kyoto on May 27, 2002. Just before and after my birth, I spent some while time in Kyoto, my mother's hometown. Most of my life has been spent in a place called Amami Oshima in Kagoshima Prefecture. It is located between Kagoshima and Okinawa prefectures and is often mistaken for Okinawa. I went to school in Amami all the way from kindergarten to high school, and then moved to Tokyo last year when I became a SWU student.

My Past Study Abroad

When I was in the second year of junior high school, I participated in an exchange program held by Amami City. I went to Texas, USA. I stayed with a host family for two weeks, and I really enjoyed going to school and spending time with my host family.

I went to New York City in the United States during the winter vacation of my second year of high school. I was very nervous because I went alone, unlike the time I went to Texas. However, I was able to make friends from overseas who I met at the language school, and we went to Manhattan together a lot and had a very fun two weeks. It was a precious experience to be able to enjoy the United States during Christmas and New Year.

Ante interdum

This is the SWU garden. There are benches around a big tree, round in shape and very relaxing. It is illuminated at night, and is especially beautiful in winter with snow.

This is the cafeteria. I often see students eating here, especially in the summer. Even though this university is located in the city, it is a place where you can experience nature.

This is the street next to the "British School in Tokyo," a school located inside Showa Women's University. There are many students from the British School here during lunch time. This is where you can experience the atmosphere of being abroad.
